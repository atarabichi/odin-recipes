Cheeseburger

Description

A cheeseburger containing lettuce, tomatoe and onions.

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onions
  • Meat

Steps:

  1. Grab two buns from a bag of buns
  2. Fry a hamburger patty for roughly a few minutes
  3. Add cheese to the top of the patty while still frying
  4. Sprinkle salt & pepper on top of uncooked burger
  5. Add lettuce, tomatoe, onions to the top of the patty
  6. Add any condiments to the patty
  7. Enjoy your juicy burger!
