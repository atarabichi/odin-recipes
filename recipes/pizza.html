Pizza

Description

A cheese pizza contains cheese, flour, dough, tomato sauce, salt and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Flour
  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Use the flour to make dough
  2. Flatten the dough into a 12"-18" circle
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the entire surface of dough
  4. Sprinkle salt & pepper on top of uncooked pizza
  5. Layer shredded cheese all over the pizza
  6. Preheat oven to 350F for 15 minutes
  7. Place pizza in the oven for 25-30 minutes
