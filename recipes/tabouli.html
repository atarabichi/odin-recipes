Tabouli

Description

A mediterranean salad containing many fresh vegetables. A personal favorite of mine!

Ingredients:

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Parsley
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Slice and dice tomatoes, onions
  2. Chop lettuce
  3. Chop parsley
  4. Mix together
  5. Add salt & 1/4 cup olive oil
  6. Mix thoroughly (again)
  7. Enjoy your fresh tabouli!
